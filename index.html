Michael Lombardo

Principal Investigator

I direct the Laboratory for Autism and Neurodevelopmental Disorders (LAND) based in the Center for Neuroscience and Cognitive Systems (CNCS) at the Italian Institute of Technology (IIT) in Rovereto, Italy. IIT LAND’s translational research program is focused on understanding heterogeneity and early developmental mechanisms involved in autism and atypical neurodevelopment.
